**Glabal Kerboard Accelerator:
  Ctrl-F1: help
  Ctrl-F2: record
  Ctrl-F3: setting
  Ctrl-F4: dict
  Ctrl-F5: about
  Ctrl-Q , Alt-X : Quit
  Alt-Z: Iconify

**Main window's Kerboard Accelerator:
  help: F1
  record: F2
  setting: F3
  dict: F4
  about: F5

  choosebook: C , 1
  firstrecite: F, 2
  revise: R, 3
  shooting: S, 4
  typing: T, 5
  rest: E, 6
  know: W, 7

ho22bus is basicly a fork of the famous "reciteword", which is not
able to compile and run on a modern GNU/Linux distribution. The
Maintainer sufferred some failures on CET-6, so is planing to bring 
reciteword back to life, and... Here it is!
